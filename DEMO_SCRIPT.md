## ⚡ THE AMAZE FACTORS (Tech Differentiators)
*Mention these specifically to win over judges:*

1.  **The "Silence" Algorithm:** We don't optimize for engagement; we optimize for *utility per interruption*. Most AIs are desperate to talk; AURA is desperate to stay silent.
2.  **Festina Lente (PRISM Gate):** A decision-theoretic framework that computes social burden in real-time.
3.  **Shadow AURA:** A parallel counterfactual simulation that asks: *"If I stay silent now, what is the regret score in 2 hours?"*
4.  **HMAC Audit Chain:** Cryptographic accountability. Every decision AURA makes is chained so it can't be tampered with.
5.  **Adversary Veto:** A secondary safety model that tries to "break" the primary agent's logic before it speaks.
