The assistant that talks first.

Siri waits for you to ask. AURA watches your day and decides when staying silent is worse than speaking. Backed by a 2026 decision-theoretic framework. Runs on your laptop, no cloud.

Try AURA → See the brain

The Problem

Modern phones bombard you with notifications. Modern assistants do the opposite — they stay silent until you wake them. Both extremes are broken. You're either drowning in noise or getting no help when you actually need it.

The Idea

AURA computes the cost of staying silent against the burden of speaking, on every potential nudge. She speaks only when the math says staying silent would be worse. Every decision is inspectable.

📅 Watches your day

Calendar, sleep, steps, weather, location, notification load — fused into a Day-Readiness Score.

🛡 Two-AI safety

Every decision passes through the gate, the adversary, and (when borderline) Shadow AURA's counterfactual.
